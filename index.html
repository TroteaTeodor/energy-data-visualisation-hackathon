Integrations

Data sources and APIs powering this dashboard.

Live APIs
Elia Open Data ods201
opendata.elia.be
Live

Real-time electricity generation mix by fuel type, including nuclear, gas, solar, wind, and other sources. Values are provided in MW and displayed in the Overview sidebar.

Generation mix Nuclear Solar Wind MW
Elia Open Data ods134
opendata.elia.be
Live

Quarter-hourly imbalance prices in EUR/MWh, converted to EUR/kWh for display. Shown in the Energy Prices tab and used to calculate potential savings in personalised tips.

Imbalance prices EUR/kWh 15-min resolution
Fluvius Open Data 1_03
opendata.fluvius.be
Live

Street-level annual electricity consumption in kWh/year, aggregated per street segment across Belgium. Displayed in the Overview sidebar when zoomed in on the map.

Street consumption kWh/year Belgium
OpenStreetMap / Nominatim
nominatim.openstreetmap.org
Live

Address geocoding for the "My Energy Path" search feature and the location setting. Queries are restricted to Belgium.

Geocoding Belgium Address search
OpenStreetMap Power Infrastructure
openstreetmap.org · /belgium_grid.geojson
Local

Full Belgian electricity grid geometry including transmission and distribution lines. Extracted from OpenStreetMap, stored as GeoJSON, and served from our own Nginx instance. Visualised as the interactive grid layer on the Overview map.

Grid geometry GeoJSON Transmission lines Distribution lines Self-hosted
